Jessica Gumerson, Ph.D.

Originally from northern Michigan, I attended the University of Michigan for both undergraduate and graduate school and received my PhD in Molecular & Integrative Physiology. After a short postdoctoral fellowship at the University of Maryland Baltimore, I took a permanent position as a Biologist at the National Eye Institute in Bethesda. I have since moved to industry in the ocular gene therapy field and remain in the DC/Maryland area.


Curious about pursuing a career in biomedical research? You're welcome to connect with me online or reach out via email.
