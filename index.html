Jessica Gumerson, Ph.D.


Summary

Currently working as a biologist in the Neurobiology Neurodegeneration & Repair Laboratory (NNRL) at the National Eye Institute (NEI). My current focus is on the visual system and diseases such as retinitis pigmentosa. Neuromuscular and neurodegenerative disease mechanisms and therapies remain a long-standing interest. In previous labs, I studied proteins associated with muscular dystrophy and related conditions like Walker-Warburg syndrome and Muscle-Eye-Brain disease.
