Summary

I currently work as a biologist in the Neurobiology Neurodegeneration & Repair Laboratory (NNRL) at the National Eye Institute (NEI). Our research is focused on defining fundamental pathways that guide differentiation of retinal neurons, understanding how these pathways are disrupted in inherited retinal disease, and developing therapies to treat these conditions.
